# Lantern Shards

## Utility

- Reveal hidden maintenance glyphs on old machinery.
- Stabilize one failing counter for a short time.
- Bargaining leverage with factions that need the lantern operational.

## Risks

- Fragments emit light signatures detectable by patrol sensors.
- Mishandling introduces distress and disorientation.

## Story Hooks

- A missing shard sits in a flooded lockbox near the drain gate.
- A rival crew member offers a fake shard to mislead the party.
