# Setting Frame

The Drowned Gate district runs on old flood pumps and brittle alliances. Whoever controls the Iron Lantern can reroute power and pressure across the canal network.

## Core Pressure

- Infrastructure is failing.
- Rival factions are moving tonight.
- Public order depends on a system no one fully controls.

## Why the Characters Care

- Payment and leverage are immediate.
- District failure harms allies and contacts.
- Success creates long-term influence with major factions.
