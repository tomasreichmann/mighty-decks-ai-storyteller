# Warden Sable

## Public Face

Calm, disciplined, and practical. Speaks in short orders and treats every delay as sabotage.

## Agenda

- Keep the lantern out of rival hands.
- Pin any breach on outside smugglers.
- Escape with leverage if the district locks down.

## Pressure Moves

- Seal off one route with armed sentries.
- Trigger floodgate release to split the party.
- Offer a temporary deal, then betray it when clock advances.
