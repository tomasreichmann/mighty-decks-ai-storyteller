# Player Summary (No Spoilers)

The Drowned Gate district is failing, and one relic can decide who controls what survives.
Your crew has one night to infiltrate a flooded industrial zone, outmaneuver rival operators, and get out before the pressure breaks everything.

Expect:

- high-stakes heist choices
- escalating environmental danger
- faction pressure and shifting alliances
- multiple routes, each with different risk and payoff

Bring characters who can improvise under pressure and act when plans go sideways.
