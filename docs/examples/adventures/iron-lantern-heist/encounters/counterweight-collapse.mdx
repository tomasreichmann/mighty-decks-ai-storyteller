# Counterweight Collapse

## Trigger

The first loud action on the gantry causes bolts to shear and the suspended counterweight to drop.

## Stakes

- Success: party crosses with momentum and gains position.
- Mixed: crossing succeeds but someone takes a lasting cost.
- Failure/chaos: route shifts, debris blocks exits, pressure counter jumps.

## Counter Guidance

- `Collapse Clock` starts at 0/4.
- Advance by 1 each major action, by 2 on loud failures.
- At 4/4, gantry breaks and route changes permanently.

## Fail-Forward

Even on collapse, a new route opens through the maintenance pit, but with added patrol pressure.
