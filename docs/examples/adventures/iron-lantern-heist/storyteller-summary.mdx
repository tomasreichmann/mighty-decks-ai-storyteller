# Storyteller Summary (Spoilers)

The players are trying to recover the Iron Lantern before district control collapses.  
Warden Sable publicly defends the vault but privately plans to sell access once players trigger enough chaos.

## Hidden Structure

- The rival crew is bait, not the primary threat.
- Flood infrastructure is already compromised before play starts.
- The lantern shards are both key and liability: they solve access problems and attract patrol detection.

## Pressure Arc

1. Early tension: infiltration and route choice.
2. Mid tension: counterweight collapse forces costly decisions.
3. Late tension: escape path closes as flood pressure and faction response spike.

## Recommended Facilitation Focus

- Keep fail-forward routes open after setbacks.
- Let Sable's agenda advance whenever players hesitate.
- Use consequences to reshape route options, not stop play.
