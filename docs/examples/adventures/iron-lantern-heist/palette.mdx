# Palette

## Dos

- Keep pressure visible and rising with counters.
- Reward risky teamwork and creative stunts.
- Let failures move the story forward with cost.
- Keep location details concrete and tactile.

## Donts

- Do not hard-stop progress behind one clue.
- Do not resolve all conflict through direct combat.
- Do not remove player agency with hidden auto-fail.
- Do not over-explain mechanics in narration.
