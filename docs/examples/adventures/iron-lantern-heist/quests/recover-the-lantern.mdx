# Recover the Iron Lantern

## Hooks

- A blackout contract offers payment for the lantern.
- A friendly mechanic warns that the district pumps will fail without it.
- A rival crew leaks patrol shift codes for one night.

## Node Sketch

- `node-sluice-breach` (entry): break into flood control wing.
- `node-cable-run` (entry): cross upper cables above patrols.
- `node-vault-access`: unlock or bypass lantern chamber.
- `node-counterweight-collapse`: survive collapsing gantry.
- `node-escape-floodgate` (conclusion): choose escape route and consequences.

## Conclusion Variants

- Clean extraction with faction favor.
- Costly extraction with injury and new enemy.
- Lantern secured but district damage shifts future politics.
