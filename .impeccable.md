## Design Context

### Users
Players, storytellers, and content authors working in a local-first tabletop storytelling app. They use the interface to create adventures, run sessions, author module content, and test UI patterns in the styleguide.

### Brand Personality
Playful, tactile, and storybook-like with a strong retro comic-book flavor. The interface should feel bold and handcrafted, not generic SaaS. Emotional goals: delight, clarity, and confidence.

### Aesthetic Direction
Lean into a vivid vintage tabletop/comic aesthetic with warm textures, chunky borders, expressive labels, and high-contrast surfaces. Prefer intentional asymmetry, layered depth, and a handmade feel over flat minimalism.

### Design Principles
- Make primary actions obvious and tactile.
- Keep labels, controls, and helper text tightly grouped.
- Use vertical rhythm and spacing to guide attention.
- Preserve the handcrafted comic tone in form controls and labs.
- Favor legibility and alignment over decorative noise.
