# Siege of Rock Bottom

💡 This encounter gives players an opportunity to choose a side in the conflict to gain spoils from the other side, gaining relations bonus with one and loosing with the other. A 3rd option is to try to retrieve the relic from the station under fire and return it for peaceful resolution and relations bonus with both.

_You jump into a system ablaze with conflict. Your sensors are flooded with comms chatter and weapons fire. A flotilla of decorated Machinist priest vessels is laying siege to a massive industrial station, "Rock Bottom." Bright pulses of laser score the station's rusty plating, while scrappy <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" /> ships and automated turrets return fire._

_A priority channel cuts through the noise: **"This is the vessel *Cog's Righteous Fury*. The heretics of Rock Bottom harbor a stolen Machinist relic. Surrender it, and we will cease this purification. Resist, and be cleansed by holy fire."** Another voice, laced with static and defiance, retorts: **"We don't have your shitty trinket, you zealots! Piss off before we make your ships see-through!"**_

_The battle rages, and your unexpected arrival has not gone unnoticed. Both sides are hailing you, demanding you declare your allegiance or be treated as hostile. This is a chance to make powerful allies, or a great deal of profit._

## Choose Your Side

🎭 **The <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" /> and <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" /> both demand your help. What do you do?**

### 1. Side with the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" />

_You answer the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" />' call, your weapons turning on the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" /> vessels. The priests welcome your aid in their holy quest._

-   **Objective:** Help the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" /> disable Rock Bottom's defenses and board the station.
-   **Challenge:** The station's turrets and remaining <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" /> ships fight desperately. Overcome a **Challenge** equal to **3x the number of players**. At the end of each round, you have to defend from attacks worth **1x Effect** per player.
-   **Reward:**
    -   🔎 The <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" />, victorious, allow you to salvage parts from the defeated <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" /> ships.
    -   🏺 Gain **1x Weapon system of your choice**.
    -   🤝 **+2 Relations** with the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" />, **-2 Relations** with <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" />.
-   **Failure:** If you flee without completing the objective, the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" /> are displeased with your performance. 🤝 **-1 Relations** with the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" />.

### 2. Side with the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" />
_You align with the underdogs, opening fire on the Machinist vessels. The <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" /> cheer your name over the comms._
-   **Objective:** Help the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" /> repel the Machinist attack.
-   **Challenge:** The Machinist ships are technologically superior and well-coordinated. Overcome a **Challenge** equal to **3x the number of players**.
-   **Reward:**
    -   🔎 The <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" />, grateful for your help, give you access to their hidden stockpiles as thanks.
    -   🏺 Each player gains a **Modified Weapon or Armor**.
    -   🤝 **+2 Relations** with <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" />, **-2 Relations** with the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" />.
-   **Failure:** If you flee without completing the objective, the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" /> curse your incompetence. 🤝 **-1 Relations** with <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" />.

### 3. Retrieve the Relic
_You try to broadcast a message of peace, offering to mediate, but the fighting does not cease and you operate under heavy fire. You try to board the station, and find the stolen relic, and return it to the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" /> to end the bloodshed._
-   **Objective:** Infiltrate the station under fire, locate the relic, and return it. This is a multi-stage challenge.
-   **Stage 1: Board the station:** The station is on high alert. You need to fly through cross-fire to get to a docking port.
    -   💡 **Challenge: 2x Players**. Sustain 1 damage to a random ship system for every missing Effect.
-   **Stage 2: Find the relic:** You detect the relic, an ancient data-reading device labeled "<GameCard type="AssetCard" slug="biskma" />," in possession of a drunk pirate fighting Machinist droids. Get in and retrieve it.
    -   💡 **Challenge: 2x Players**. The player crew sustains 1 damage to a random member for every missing Effect.
-   **Reward:**
    -   🔎 You return the relic. The <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" /> are overjoyed, and the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" /> are relieved. Both sides see you as honorable mediators.
    -   🏺 The <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" /> reward you with a sacred schematic, and the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" /> offer a token of their gratitude. Gain **1x new Ship System** and **2x Resources**.
    -   🤝 **+2 Relations** with the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="machinist-church" />, **+2 Relations** with <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" />.
-   **Failure:**
    -   If you flee without completing the objective, both sides take you for a coward. 🤝 **-1 Relations** with both factions.
