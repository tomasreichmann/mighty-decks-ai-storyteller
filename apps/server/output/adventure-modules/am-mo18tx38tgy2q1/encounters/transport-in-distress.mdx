# Transport in Distress

💡 This is an introductory ship-to-ship combat encounter. The players can choose to engage the pirate ship, which is already damaged. The goal is to defeat or disable the pirate vessel.
💡 If it starts to drag, let the signs of the <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="void-horror" /> show up, like frosting and rippling space. The pirates will get spooked and try to flee and so should the players.

_You jumped to the coordinates of the distress call and see a <GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" />' transport entering a nearby asteroid field followed by a pirate corvette. Both ships seem pretty beat up. The transport's weapons and comms seem to be offline. They are trying to charge their Spin drive to jump away, but the disruptor fire from the pirate ship keeps draining its energy._

-   The transport is damaged and trying to escape through the asteroid field. Their comms seem to be offline.
-   🔎 **Comms:**
    -   Contacting the transport reveals their comms array is down.
    -   Contacting the pirates reveals they are a couple of beat-up humanoid bugs called the _Xithrax_ commandeering a stolen decommissioned corvette, looking for easy prey.
-   If you want to engage, you will have to move to the asteroid field. Navigating it requires a skilled pilot, you risk damaging the ship, but you can also shield yourself from enemy fire.
-   The pirates seem to be ignoring you for now.
-   🔎 **Sensors:**
    -   Effect 1: The pirates are armed with a disruptor and a laser array, their reactor seems to be damaged so they are alternating between them.
    -   Effect 2: Their sensors seem to be also damaged
    -   Effect 3: There are several life signs that don't appear to be moving. They are running a skeleton crew.
    -   Weapon station Mk II armed with.
        -   <GameCard type="AssetCard" slug="disruptor-mk-i" />: De-powers a system by 1PU for 1 turn.
        -   Laser array Mk I: Damages a system by 1 Damage.
    -   You see the pirate ship's layout.
-   📅 If the crew attacks the pirates, they turn to engage your ship and the transport jumps away next turn.
    _You have a feeling the **<GameCard type="ActorCard" slug="smugglers" />** will remember your help._

-   If the pirate ship generator is destroyed the ship explodes.
