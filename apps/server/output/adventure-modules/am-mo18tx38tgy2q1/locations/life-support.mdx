# Life Support

![](/api/adventure-artifacts/life-support-7cc06593453ccad5dd9c.png)

## Introduction

Manning this station, you can re-pressure or de-pressure any room on the ship, control the temperature onboard and of the shower water, and reusing of waste water.

## Description

    -   🏺 There is are 2 **Spin batteries** (Pushing Surge) lying on the floor. You can use these to fully charge the Spin drive and jump away. They are very expensive and damaging to the spin drive, only to be used as the last resort.
    -   🔎 de-pressuring is useful in case of a fire or hostile boarding.
    -   🔎 Water is not running at the moment. The water in the boiler seems to be frozen solid.
