# Weapon Station

![](/api/adventure-artifacts/weapons-station-436da3cb55bbcb0d221c.png)

## Introduction

You can fire the ship's weapons from here.

## Description

    -   All but one of the weapon systems were uninstalled. A single laser array is left, but it is malfunctioning.
    -   Manning this station can increase damage or fire rate.
    -   📅 As you investigated the malfunctioning laser, there was a buildup of energy from a damaged capacitor and the resulting spark ignited wiring hanging from the ceiling. The fire is quickly spreading.
    -   🔎 If repaired, you can use it to target a specific room on the enemy ship. It causes 1 Damage to target system if not absorbed by shield in exchange for 1PU.
    -   🔎 Repairing requires 1 Spare part.
