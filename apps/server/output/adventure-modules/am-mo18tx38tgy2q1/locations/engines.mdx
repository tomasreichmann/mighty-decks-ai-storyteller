# Engines

![](/api/adventure-artifacts/engine-room-e8d353054b6ec0d933a4.png)

## Introduction

They are un-powered. They need 1PU to provide thrust to move around and maneuver during fights.

## Description

    -   Even with these engines operational, you are so far from anything, it will take years before you reach anything worth the effort.
    -   Manning this station can provide extra speed.
    -   They can be upgraded to provide advantage over ships with weaker engines.
    -   🔎 When the engines are running, they sometimes pick up a strange echo, like when flying through a magnetic field. But you are pretty far away from anything that can have one.
